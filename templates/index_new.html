HOW IT WORKS

MAKE A PROPOSAL

Tell the world what you want to work on: how often, for how long, and how much it will cost. Throw in any video, pictures, audio, text, or files you need to tell your story. Submit it for the public's approval.

CROSS THE THRESHHOLD

Every proposal has a minimum funding threshhold. If enough money is pledged within 30 days, it immediately converts to funded. Then, money is withdrawn from the pledgers accounts and put in escrow. Any further pledges are immediately added to escrow after this point.

WORK AND GET PAID

Once your proposal becomes a funded project it's time to get to work. If you specified an upfront cash requirement it gets disbursed right away. After this point, you get paid a chunk of what's in escrow every time you post content to your project.

TRANSPARENCY

Indiepen tracks who puts in contributions of money and content to each project and publicizes that history when the content is published. It's impossible to eliminate bias, but in this way we hope to expose it so that your audience can make up their own mind who to trust.
