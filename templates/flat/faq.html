Who Owns The Content

Content funded through indiepen remains owned by its creator, however, a percentage of ownership is permanently liscensed to indiepen. This is done to allow indiepen to handle any future liscensing of content to third parties.

What Fees Are Involved

Indiepen takes an industry standard 5% of any transaction to cover our server and engineering costs. In addition, 2.9% + 30Â¢ of each transaction must be taken in order to cover the fees of our payment processor, Stripe.

Why Was My Project Not Approved

Indiepen staff explicitly reserves for itself curatorial, not editorial power. This means that we go out of our way to not make any decisions about what content should or should not be allowed on the site. However, sometimes legal restrictions force us to reject a proposal. In such an event we will work with you to create a proposal that we can allow on the site.
